Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password?
    Go to Settings > Account Security and follow the instructions to change your password.
  • How can I contact my instructor?
    You can contact your instructor via the messaging feature in the Courses section or through their university email.
  • Where do I find my assignment deadlines?
    All assignment deadlines are available in the Assignments page and shown on your Calendar.
  • How do I submit an assignment?
    Go to the Assignments page, select the relevant assignment, and use the Submit button to upload your work.
